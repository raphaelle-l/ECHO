Meet
Echo.

Echo is a musician living in the "poverty apartments" of Notting. She plays everything. Drums, bass, guitar, keys. Talent isn't the problem. Rent is.

Turns out guitars and rent cost money. So Echo's picked up a… unique side hustle.

Now she moves through the city on rollerblades, leaving spray-painted tags and the occasional empty bank vault behind her. Somewhere between a punk show and a crime scene, Echo is trying to make it big. Or at least make rent.

"Everyone has a side hustle,
mine's just
more fun."
